Strawberry Cupcake

Description:

With this creative but impressive cupcake recipe you will wow anyone and everyone!

It's a really sweet, strawberry flavored, oven cupcake recipe.

Ingredients:

To make these wonderful cupcakes you will need:

  • An egg yolk
  • 6g of Flour
  • 6 Strawberries
  • 8g of Edible Glitter

Steps:

To cook these breathtaking cupcakes you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Put it all on a bowl and thoroughly mix
  2. Place the mix on your preferred cupcake mold
  3. Place in the oven at 200ยบ C for 30'
  4. Just wait until they cool off, and you're done!
